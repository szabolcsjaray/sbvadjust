YouTube SBV helper - Translation tutorial


This page shows step-by-step how to make a subtitle in another language.

Step 1

Open caption tool (Youtube SBV Helper) page. It will show the last state, as it was closed.

Step 2

Load the English subtitle from a saved file in the top left box with the load button.

Step 3

You see the English subtitle loaded in the top left text box.
Now switch to "Translation" (if it is not already set), by selecting it from the drop-down.

Step 4

Load the English subtitle in the work area, by clicking on the Load [1] (next to the Translation).
You will see the English text boxes loaded in the workspace [2].

Step 5

If we have auto translated, rough translation sbv, it can be loaded to the big text box with Load [1] button.

Step 6

You will see the loaded rough tranmslation in big text box, like this.

Step 7

Now load it to the editor text boxes in the workplace by clicking on << [1].
You will see the rough translation appearing in the editor boxes of the workplace [2].

Step 8

It's time to fix, correct the rough translation now. Click in the editor boxes, fix, what is needed, go via the whole video.

Step 9

After the subtitle is ready, you can click on the Show button [1], to see it as a SBV file. If you think, it is OK, click on Save [2], otherwise you can close this, and go back fixing some more.

Step 10

Give the file a proper name [1], and save it the usual way [2].

Ok, you are done, you can close the tool webpage. ✅
